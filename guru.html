#Bhagavad Gita

      श्रीभगवानुवाच:
      तद्विद्धि प्रणिपातेन परिप्रश्नेन सेवया ।
      उपदेक्ष्यन्ति ते ज्ञानं ज्ञानिनस्तत्त्वदर्शिनः ॥4.34
      
      

      Translation:
      learn the truth by approaching a spiritual master. Inquire from them submissively and render service unto them.
      The self-realized souls can impart knowledge to you because they have seen the truth.

      

      #Srimad Bhagavatam

      नैषां मतिस् तावदुरुक्रमाङ्घ्रिं
      स्पृशत्यनर्थापगमो यदर्थः ।
      महीयसां पादरजोऽभिषेकं
      निष्किञ्चनानां न वृणीत यावत् ॥7.5.32 
      

      

      Translation:
      Unless one bathes in the dust of the lotus feet of a pure devotee (Guru), one's mind cannot touch the lotus feet of the
      Lord, which are the true shelter from all troubles.
